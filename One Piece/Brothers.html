Brothers

Sharing three cup of sake, Luffy,Ace and Sabo became brothers when they were kids. In order to pursue their dreams they have to set sail to the sea.
Sabo was the first one to set sail at the age of 10.

After Sabo left, Luffy and Ace promised to set sail at the age of 17.

Ace was second to pursue his dream and began his journey.

Luffy was the last one to chase his dream at the age of 17. He set sail with the ambition of becoming King Of The Pirates aka Pirate King.

These brothers will soon be infamous and will be known across the grand line.
